Battlefield Rules & Regulations

Equal Opportunity

  • An equal opportunity implies that We have no prejudice against any particpants. However participants cannot be below SS1 and over Ist year of Undergraduate studies.
  • Students are advised to compete in any of our 8 sublects that they are most proficient in.

    • Malpractice

  • We higly discourage all forms of malpractice in the battlefield as all selected participants must undergo physical authentication tests before qualified participants can recieve their academic grants.
  • Authentication Tests usually require a selected participant to score not less than a minimum of 8% of the score that qualified them for an academic grant.

    • Participant Privacy Policy

  • It is imperative for participants to understand that the battlefield is a Real-Time event and Topscorers are updated in real-time based on higher scores updated.
  • For a participant to compete in this event, They agree that top scores are accessible to the public in real-time @ www.nab.live
  • All participants deciding to enroll into this event agree that our data-analytics will be made accessible to The Federal Government.
  • All participants in the sciences hereby agree that their performance evaluation statistics will be made available to the Federal Ministry of Science and Technology as all top scorers in this field will be reccomended as Nationally deployable assets in human capital.
  • All participants in the humanities and arts hereby agree that their analytics and performance will be made available to the Federal Ministry of Education as all top scorers in this field will be reccomended as Nationally deployable assets in human capital.
